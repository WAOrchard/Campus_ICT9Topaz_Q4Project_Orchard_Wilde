Welcome to the Philippines!

travel guide

  1. Mayon Volcano


    • an active stratovolcano known for its perfect cone shape
    • Location: Albay, Bicol Region, Philippines
    • Price: Free
  1. Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park


    • a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rich marine biodiversity
    • Location: Sulu Sea, Philippines
    • Timings: Accessible only via liveaboard diving trips during March to June
  1. Vigan


    • a well-preserved Spanish colonial town with cobblestone streets
    • Location: Ilocos Sur, Philippines
    • Price: Free
  1. Taal Volcano


    • an active volcano situated on an island within a lake
    • Location: Batangas, Philippines
    • Price: 100 PHP per head
  1. Enchanted River


    • a stunning, crystal-clear river known for its deep blue waters
    • Location: Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur, Philippines
    • Price: 30 PHP per head
  1. Hundred Islands National Park


    • a beautiful hotel known for its natural beauty and adventure activities
    • Location: Alaminos, Pangasinan, Philippines
    • Price: 80 PHP per head
