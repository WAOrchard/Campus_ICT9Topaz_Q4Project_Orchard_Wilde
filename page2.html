Noli Me Tangere Characters

Character Description Image
Crisostomo Ibarra The protagonist, a young and wealthy Filipino who returns from Europe to reform his country.
María Clara Ibarra's fiancée, loved by all, she is the most prominent female character.
Padre Damaso A Franciscan friar and the main antagonist, known for his arrogance and abuse of power.
Padre Salvi A cunning and manipulative priest who harbors ill intentions towards Ibarra.
Elias A revolutionary character who becomes Ibarra's friend.
Pilosopo Tasyo An old scholar and philosopher who is considered eccentric by the townspeople.
Sisa A deranged woman searching for her missing sons, Crispin and Basilio.
Basilio Sisa's elder son, who witnesses the cruelties inflicted upon his family.
Crispin Sisa's younger son, who is falsely accused of theft and disappears under suspicious circumstances.
Capitán Tiago María Clara's father, a wealthy and influential figure in the town.
Donya Victorina A Filipina woman who excessively emulates Spanish customs and looks down on her fellow Filipinos.
Don Tiburcio Donya Victorina's husband, a Spaniard who is often dominated by his wife.
